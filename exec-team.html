The GMU Exec Team!

Here, you can learn all about GMU's current executives! Much like all of our members, each executive on our team brings a creative, unique set of skills, experience, and passion to our game-development culture! We have four distinct executive categories for you to check out: Directors, Development Team, Operations, and Film Crew!

To learn more about how exactly each executive member contributes to our team, or if you'd like to learn more about each executive category in general, check out our sections below!

Last Updated: 2024-11-03

DIRECTORS

Our directors are responsible for managing the departments and ensuring cohesive operations. They help maintain communication across departments, organize meetings, and oversee quality control. Assistant Directors assist the Main Director in administrative tasks.

DEVELOPMENT TEAM

Our development team is responsible for running student-led tutorials and workshops. They guide students throughout the game development process and have share their insights with their corresponding departments, as well as engage in cross-department communication to keep everyone informed and up to date with the development's progression.

OPERATIONS TEAM

The operations team plan, coordinate, and manage events, handling logistics, budgeting, and day-of operations to create engaging experiences for the members of GMU.

FILM CREW

The Film Crew captures GMU's journey from pitch to product. The videos we produce showcase the talent, creativity, and hard work of our members to the world!
