The GMU Exec Team!

Here, you can learn all about GMU's current executives! Much like all of our members, each executive on our team brings a creative, unique set of skills, experience, and passion to our game-development culture! We have four distinct executive categories for you to check out: Directors, Development Team, Operations, and Film Crew!

To learn more about how exactly each executive member contributes to our team, or if you'd like to learn more about each executive category in general, check out our sections below!

Last Updated: 2024-10-25

DIRECTORS

Our directors are responsible for all aspects of our team.

DEVELOPMENT TEAM

Our development team is responsible for creating tutorials and guiding students throughout the game development process!

OPERATIONS TEAM

Our operations team are responsible for maintaining and improving the underlying day-to-day operations of GMU throughout the year.

FILM CREW

Our film crew is responsible for producing and editing all of our films! This includes editing, production, post-production and more!
